A qualifying dance studio is an established, offline-first business that:

- Is growing and hitting operational complexity — managing schedules, payments, and rosters manually or semi-manually (Excel, spreadsheets, basic accounting tools like Xero/QuickBooks)

This is NOT a startup. It's a real, functioning dance business that is scaling and facing operational chaos.

## ANALYZE BY ANSWERING THESE QUESTIONS:

Answer each question based on the homepage content before making the final decision.

**Q1 — Is this a dance studio?**
Does the site belong to a dance school, dance academy, or dance studio that teaches classes?
- YES: mentions dance classes, dance styles (ballet, hip-hop, contemporary, jazz, ballroom, Latin, etc.), enrolling students, class schedules
- PARTIAL: mentions dance but primarily sells performances, events, or competitions only
- NO: gym, yoga, fitness center, martial arts, or unrelated business

**Q2 — How many locations?**
- "1 location" — single studio address, no mention of other branches
- "2-5 locations" — mentions multiple studios, branches, campuses
- "5+ locations" — franchise, large chain, 5+ branches
- "unknown" — no location info

**Q3 — How many students?**
Look for any numbers: enrolled students, active members, students per year, classes per week × avg class size.
- "under_200" — explicitly small (e.g. "boutique studio", small class sizes, 1-2 teachers only)
- "200-500" — medium-sized signals
- "500+" — explicitly large numbers, many programs, many teachers, years of operation with scale
- "unknown" — no size signals

**Q4 — Age groups served?**
- "kids_only" — only children's classes
- "adults_only" — only adult classes
- "mixed" — both kids and adults
- "unknown" — unclear

**Q5 — Operations signals — do they seem to manage things manually?**
Look for signals of manual/semi-manual operations:
- MANUAL signals: no mention of online booking system, mentions "contact us to register", email/phone registration, simple contact form only, no member portal
- SEMI-DIGITAL: mentions basic online booking or payment, but no sophisticated system
- DIGITAL: clearly uses a dedicated studio management platform (mentions specific software, automated scheduling, parent portal, etc.)
- UNKNOWN: not enough signals

**Q6 — Growth signals — is this business actively growing or scaling?**
- YES: new locations opening, new programs launching, expanding team, "join our growing community", mentions years of growth
- MAYBE: stable established business, no clear growth signals but well-established
- NO: single small operation, no growth signals
